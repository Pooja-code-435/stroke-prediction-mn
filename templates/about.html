Overview

Our Health Prediction System is a machine learning-powered web application designed to provide early insights into various health conditions. By analyzing user input data, it predicts potential health risks and encourages timely medical attention.

Key Features

  • Prediction models for heart disease, cancer, diabetes, and more.
  • User-friendly interface with secure data handling.
  • Instant predictions based on medical datasets.
  • Educational insights and recommendations.

Our Mission

We aim to assist individuals in understanding their health status better through accessible and intelligent tools. While not a replacement for professional diagnosis, our system serves as a first step toward proactive healthcare.
